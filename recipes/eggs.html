Scrambled Egg Recipe

Scrambled eggs is a go to meal for a lot of people in the morning, but if not done correctly you're eggs can be dry and bland.

This scrambled egg recipe is simpe and will not dissapoint! Your eggs will be more flavourful then ever and they'll have that much needed kick to it.

Ingredients

  • 4 raw eggs
  • Tablespoon of oil
  • Hot sauce
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper

Instructions

  1. First start off by pouring one table spoon of oil onto your pan (we recommend advocado oil)
  2. Then place your pan on the stove and turn the heat on to a medium setting
  3. As you wait for the pan to heat up, you can begin working on your eggs
  4. Start by cracking all 4 of your eggs into a bowl and combining it with a pinch of salt, and pepper
  5. Then proceed to whisk until fully combined
  6. Once the pan begins to sizzle, and your eggs are ready, slowly pour your eggs onto the pan
  7. Mix your eggs around so they cook evenly
  8. Once the eggs are fluffy and fully cooked, you can remove them off the heat and top them off with your favourite hot sauce
  9. Enjoy!
