Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description of the dish

Secondary description

Ingredients

  • 4 raw eggs
  • Tablespoon of oil
  • Hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Pour one table spoon of oil onto your pan
  2. Turn the heat on a medium setting
  3. Once the oil begins to sizze, crack your eggs into the pan
  4. Mix your eggs until they are fully combined
  5. Keep cooking until the eggs are fully cooked and fluffy
  6. Then turn off the heat and add your favourite hot sauce
  7. Enjoy!
