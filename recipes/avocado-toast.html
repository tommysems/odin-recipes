Avocado Toast

Description

Eating healthier has never been easier until now! This simple healthy recipe takes less then 10 minutes to make and it tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • A teaspoon of butter
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • A pinch of chili flakes
  • A teaspoon of olive oil
  • A teaspoon of balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. First start off by spreading 1/2 teaspoon of butter on each slice of bread
  2. Then place the bread in the toaster for about 2-3 minutes or until the crust is crispy
  3. Next place your avocado in a bowl and add a pinch of salt and pepper
  4. Mix until everything is fully combined and your avocado is light and fluffy
  5. Then spread your avocado on your toast bread
  6. Add your chili flakes, a drizzle of olive oil and a drizzle of balsamic vinegar on top of the avocado
  7. Enjoy your meal!
