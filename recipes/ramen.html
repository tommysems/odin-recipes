Easy Homemade Cheesy Ramen Recipe

This simple recipe will change the way that you eat instant ramen forever. With the addition of a few simple ingredients, your instant ramen will be more flavourful then ever and it won't taste like it had just come out of a package.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Mr. Noodles instant ramen
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 100mL of milk
  • 2 slices of kraft american cheese
  • 10mL of sriracha sauce

Instructions

  1. First start off by cooking your ramen noodles according to the instructions labeled on the package
  2. Then drain your noodles and set them aside
  3. To begin making the sauce, start by adding 1 tablespoon of butter to a pan and place it on a medium-low heat
  4. Once all the butter is melted, add 100mL of milk to the pan
  5. Mix it around for about a minute and then add your 2 slices of cheese and hot sauce to the pan
  6. Stir until fully combined
  7. Then proceed to add your ramen to the sauce and mix until all the ramen is drenched in sauce
  8. Get out your chopsticks and enjoy!
